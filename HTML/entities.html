ENTITIES PLAY AN IMPORTANT ROLE IN HTML

FOR EXAMPLE, THE LESS THAN SIGN (<) IS USED TO OPEN TAGS, SO IF YOU WANT TO DISPLAY A LESS THAN SIGN AS TEXT, YOU NEED TO USE THE ENTITY <.

HERE ARE SOME COMMON HTML ENTITIES:

  • & - Ampersand (&)
  • < - Less than (<)
  • > - Greater than (>)
  • " - Double quote (")
  • ' - Single quote (')
  • © - Copyright (©)
  • ® - Registered (®)
  • € - Euro (€)
  • £ - Pound (£)
  • ¥ - Yen (¥)
